Our Journey and Values

Harbour Fleet started as a dream in 2000, born from a deep love for Sydney's stunning harbour and a passion for creating unforgettable experiences on the water. As a family-run business, we’ve grown by staying true to our core values of respect, honesty, and excellence.

  • We believe in treating every guest like family. From your first enquiry to the moment you step off the boat, we’re committed to making your experience exceptional.
  • Honesty guides everything we do. Our goal is to be the most reliable and transparent boat hire company in Sydney, offering only the best vessels and services for your special occasions.
  • Excellence isn’t just a goal, it’s our standard. Our fleet is meticulously maintained, ensuring every voyage is a reflection of our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
  • We believe in sharing the gift of knowledge. Our team is passionate about Sydney’s harbour, and we’re here to make your experience both enjoyable and enlightening.

From the early days of our family’s first boat to becoming one of Sydney’s premier boat hire companies, our journey has been driven by a love for the sea and a desire to share Sydney’s beauty with others. We look forward to welcoming you aboard and helping you create your own unforgettable harbour story.
