About Harbour Fleet

Who we are

Harbour Fleet is Sydney’s leading luxury boat hire service, offering unforgettable experiences on the water since 2000. What began as a small family-owned operation with just a few vessels has grown into a premier fleet of over 140 boats, supported by a dedicated team of skilled captains and staff. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, treating every client with respect, and building lasting relationships. Our professionalism and dedication ensure a smooth experience, even when navigating the most challenging waters.
